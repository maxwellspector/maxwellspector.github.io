About Me

I run my life by continuous improvement - optimizing for more time doing the things I love. Most people see this in my work as a Sr. Product Manager for Act-On or my former Product roles at Webtrends and Janrain.

My non-working hours are often spent with family or working on pet projects that involve computers, amateur photography, woodworking and homebrewing. When I'm outside I like to be rowing on the river or tromping through the woods with my wife, son and our dog.
