About Me

Let me introduce myself.

I have a deeply curious personality and constantly seek knowledge in a wide range of interests including technology, politics, cooking, science, and more. Technology

As I came of age at the turn of the millenium I see the forward march of technology as a measure of our civilaztion's growing sophistication. Moreover I've always been excited to experience the cutting edge. So as an early adopter and long-time beta tester, I follow the technology landscape and seek to look ahead how each advancement might improve my life, society, and feed future innovation. In my own life, that means playing with computers and IoT devices, tinkering with RaspberryPi and the like, and ejoying the emersion of video games. To stay in the know, I use Feedly to read multiple news sources on this subject and many others.

News

Facts and information are more important than ever. As a reaction to the nefarious questioning of facts and intentional distribution of lies for power and money, I've only increased my attention to news on current events, politics, science and technology. There are so many challenging problems out there in the world and being well informed is the only way I can see someone being able to even think about how to solve them. Here in Portland, we are lucky to have Oregon Public Broadcasting and I've found it and other public broadcasting stations to be the fairest and deeply informative source for basic news and current events.
